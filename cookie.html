This Cookie Policy explains how Saryaan uses cookies and similar technologies when users visit our website.

1

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on a user's device when visiting a website. They enable websites to recognize user devices, store preferences, and improve browsing functionality.

2

How Saryaan Uses Cookies

Saryaan uses cookies to:

Ensure the website functions properly

Improve site performance and reliability

Analyze user interactions and website traffic

Enhance user experience and personalization

Support security and fraud prevention mechanisms

3

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for basic website functionality such as navigation and access to secure areas.

Performance and Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with the website by collecting anonymous usage data.

Functional Cookies

Remember user preferences such as language or display settings to improve the browsing experience.

Security Cookies

Help detect suspicious activity and protect against unauthorized access.

4

Managing Cookies

Users can manage or disable cookies through their browser settings. Most browsers allow users to block or delete cookies.

⚠️ Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the website.

5

Third-Party Cookies

Saryaan may use third-party analytics or performance services that place cookies on user devices to collect usage insights. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

6

Updates to This Policy

Saryaan may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect legal, operational, or technological changes. The latest version will always be available on this page.
