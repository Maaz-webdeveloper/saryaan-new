Home › Privacy Policy

Saryaan ("Saryaan", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of personal and business information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you access our website, products, services, or platforms.

1

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When users interact with Saryaan services, request demonstrations, or submit forms, we may collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, company name, designation, and other relevant contact details.

Business and Operational Information

In connection with our services and partnerships, Saryaan may collect business-related data, including company details, transaction-related information, financial documentation, and operational information necessary to provide our services.

Technical Information

We may automatically collect technical data including IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, website usage data, and session information through cookies or similar technologies.

2

How We Use Information

Saryaan processes information for the following purposes:

To provide and maintain our services and platform functionality

To respond to inquiries, demo requests, and partnership discussions

To communicate updates, service announcements, and product information

To improve website performance, analytics, and user experience

To maintain security, detect fraud, and prevent misuse

To comply with applicable regulatory, legal, and compliance obligations

3

Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable, Saryaan processes information based on legitimate business interests, contractual necessity, user consent, and legal obligations.

4

Sharing and Disclosure of Information

✓ Saryaan does not sell personal information.

Information may be shared under the following circumstances:

With trusted service providers assisting with infrastructure, hosting, analytics, or operational support

With financial institutions, technology partners, or regulatory entities where required for service delivery

With law enforcement or government authorities where required by law or regulatory obligations

During corporate restructuring, mergers, acquisitions, or investment activities, subject to confidentiality obligations

All third parties receiving such data are required to maintain appropriate data protection and confidentiality safeguards.

5

Data Security

Saryaan implements industry-standard security practices including secure infrastructure, access control policies, encryption mechanisms, and monitoring systems designed to protect data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

While we strive to protect information, no digital transmission or storage system can guarantee absolute security.

6

Data Retention

Saryaan retains information only for the period necessary to fulfill operational, contractual, regulatory, and legal requirements. Once data is no longer required, it will be securely deleted or anonymized.

7

User Rights

Depending on applicable laws and jurisdictions, users may have rights to:

Request access to personal data

Request correction of inaccurate data

Request deletion of personal information

Restrict or object to certain data processing activities

Requests may be submitted through our official contact channels.

8

International Data Transfers

Where required, Saryaan may process or store data in jurisdictions outside the user's country of residence. Appropriate safeguards are implemented to ensure compliance with applicable data protection standards.

9

Policy Updates

Saryaan reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Questions about this policy?

Contact our team through our official channels and we will respond within 24 hours.
